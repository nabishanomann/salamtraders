Batteries & UPS

Our battery and UPS solutions feature industry-leading brands like DB, Exide, Volta, Osaka, and AGS to cover all your energy needs. From advanced Ni-CD, Ni-MH, and Li-Ion Batteries to UPS systems of every capacity, we ensure consistent, reliable power for every application.

Military Products

We provide a comprehensive range of military-grade products designed for maximum protection and efficiency. Our offerings include bulletproof jackets and helmets, all types of military batteries and chargers, wireless communication systems, and spare parts for signal equipment—ensuring safety and reliability in critical missions.

Communication Equipments

Our communication equipment solutions include wireless communication systems, intercoms, two-way radios, and satellite devices. These products are designed to ensure seamless and reliable connectivity for businesses, emergency operations, and remote environments.

Furniture & Fixtures

We provide high-quality furniture and fixtures designed to meet diverse needs with reliability and style. From office furniture like desks and chairs to specialized modular setups, our range ensures functional and aesthetic solutions for workplaces, homes, and commercial spaces.

IT, Software, & Network

Our IT solutions are designed to enhance productivity and connectivity. From laptops, desktops, printers, and scanners to biometric attendance systems, networking solutions, and software integration, we deliver end-to-end technology support tailored to your business needs.

Security Equipment

We offer a robust selection of security equipment to safeguard your premises. Our portfolio includes advanced CCTV video surveillance systems, walk-through gates, HESCO bags, hand-held scanners, and explosive detectors, ensuring comprehensive protection for high-risk environments.

Plant & Machinery

We provide a variety of plant and machinery products that combine efficiency with durability. Our offerings include electric water coolers, ceiling and pedestal fans, exhaust fans, gas heaters, room air coolers, water dispensers, and air conditioners—designed to improve comfort and functionality across various spaces.
